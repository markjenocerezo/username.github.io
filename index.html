Hi! I’m Mark Jeino Desonia Cerezo, a 22-year-old student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Engineering Technology. My creative identity is a unique blend of technical precision and artistic expression.

As a dancer and dance choreographer, I understand the beauty of rhythm and movement. Simultaneously, as a graphics and fashion designer, I translate that same energy into digital structures and wearable art.

Being the youngest in our family, I carry big and beautiful dreams—not just for myself, but for the people I love. I am driven by a deep sense of purpose and a resilient heart. No matter how tough the path becomes, I am ready to fight, adapt, and conquer every challenge that life throws my way.
